a day in the life

さくらねこ

Japanese love cats because they’re cute, clean and easier to keep as a pet. They don’t need to be walked and can go to the bathroom by themselves.

Common people also started having pet cats at home several hundred years ago and Japanese people have been involved with cats in a variety of ways since then.
